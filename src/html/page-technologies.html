backend

Backend creates complex server-side parts and logic of a product. It organizes and stores data and makes sure that the website's client side functions properly.

databases

Database is an organized collection of structured data. It helps to implement a wide range of complex business solutions.

infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the whole of the parts required for the administration and operation of the IT environment of a company and all inner processes.

automation

Automation is in charge of efficiently implementing all business operations with the least amount of human involvement.

Design is the process of creating a product's aesthetic look. A good design makes it easier for visitors to browse a website/app and ensures that business objectives are met.
